How These Forecasts Work

The system learns from your past 90 days of transactions to predict what the next 14 days will look like. Blue bars show how many customers to expect each day. The orange line shows expected revenue. Think of it like a weather forecast: tomorrow is fairly reliable, two weeks out is a rough guide. The more transaction history you build, the better these predictions become.
